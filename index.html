A Little About Me

My name is Deanna Sanchez I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer based in sunny Southern California. I've been lucky enough to work in Web Design and Development one way or another for the past 9 years. I began practicing Front-End Development on my own and as my love for development grew I knew I needed to further my skills.

So I decided to enroll in University of California, Irvine Continuing Education Division's Full-Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp and obtain my certificate. I now obtain my certifcate and am excited to say I can now build web apps! I studied: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, JQuery, Web-APIs, DOM, Ajax, NodeJS, Express, MySQL, MVC, ReactJS, MongoDB
